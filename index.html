Our Story

A Legacy of
Timeless Elegance

✦

Founded in Paris in 2010, Maison Élise was born from a simple belief: that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary. Our founder, Élise Moreau, started with a single atelier and a vision to redefine accessible luxury.

"Fashion is not just clothing — it is the language through which we tell our story to the world." — Élise Moreau, Founder

Today, our collections reach over 50 countries, blending Parisian sensibility with global craftsmanship. Every piece is ethically made and designed to last beyond seasons.
